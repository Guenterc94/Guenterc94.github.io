Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to educate novice anglers on how to properly fish for fresh water
including trout, bass, carp, and panfish without endangering them. This website will provide information along with links to videos that the user can watch to help them
learn more about fishing for trout, practicing humane fishing, and protecting the environment.

Audience

Novice fisherman that are wanting to learn how to fish for fresh water species. This site will
include tutorials, videos, and charts to help the novice fisherman learn the techniques and methods needed to fish for freshwater fish. This website will provide technical information so it is not for younger individuals.

Content

Home page

The first topic of my site will be described within the homepage; There will be an introduction to freshwater fishing (for now this will be fishing for trout). Beginner fisherman will visit this site to acquire information on how to start fishing for trout. They will come with questions on different aspects of fishing like how, when, and where to catch freshwater fish. Question they may come with may be what is the cost of fishing? What equipment do they need to start? How to handle fish when caught? How to cast? etc. This website will briefly cover all these things helping new fisherman learn the basics. On the homepage there will be a collage of trout fishing pictures with a history of trout fishing underneath. From this page visitors can then click on links to take them to either a page with info on equipment or on fishing locations within Idaho. No information will be necessary to access the various feature of this site, however there will be an email subscription section for visitors to receive news from the site.

Equipment

The 2nd page of my Site will Include information on the equipment needed for fishing. There will also be an introduction to this history of both fly-fishing gear and spin fishing gear. Visitors will be able to find info and tutorials for various aspects of fishing. Visitors will be able to access links to videos and charts. This page is mainly to introduce the fundamentals. Spin gear: This section of the webpage will introduce visitors to the different types of spinning gear available for fishing freshwater fish. This will include introducing different rods and reels, brands, tutorials, and locations to purchase gear. There will be info on different rod and reel styles, sizes, and types of fishing line. Fly gear: This section will introduce visitors to the different types of fly gear available. This will be set up like the spinning gear section. There will be info on different size rods, reels, and types of fly line. Lures: This section will discuss a broad array of different lures available on the market for freshwater fishing. There will be mini sub sections with pictures depicting lures like flies, hard lures, soft lures, bait etc. There will be brief descriptions of each lure and when to use them (times of day and seasons). Clothing: This section goes over the basic clothing available for fisherman. This will include tops, bottoms, and footwear all depending on the season.
